## 一、PET 核心不规则动词（按"易错程度"分层）

### 🔴 第一层：三形全异（最高频·最易错，必须秒反应）
> 这 20 个动词的「原形→过去式→过去分词」三个形式完全不同，PET 每次必考。

| 原形 | 过去式 | 过去分词 | 易错点 | 记忆提示 |
|------|--------|---------|--------|---------|
| **be** | was/were | **been** | been≠being | be-en 长尾 |
| **begin** | began | **begun** | begun≠began | u=完成 |
| **break** | broke | **broken** | 加-en | break-en |
| **choose** | chose | **chosen** | 加-en | cho-sen |
| **do** | did | **done** | done≠did | do-ne |
| **draw** | drew | **drawn** | 加-n | draw-n |
| **drink** | drank | **drunk** | drunk≠drank | u=完成 |
| **eat** | ate | **eaten** | 加-en | eat-en |
| **fall** | fell | **fallen** | 加-en | fall-en |
| **fly** | flew | **flown** | 加-n | flow-n |
| **forget** | forgot | **forgotten** | 双t+en | for-got-ten |
| **give** | gave | **given** | 加-en, 不双写 | gi-ven |
| **go** | went | **gone** | went≠goed | go-ne |
| **know** | knew | **known** | 加-n | know-n |
| **ride** | rode | **ridden** | 双d+en | rid-den |
| **ring** | rang | **rung** | a→u | sing/ring/swim 同型 |
| **see** | saw | **seen** | 双e | se-en |
| **sing** | sang | **sung** | a→u | 同 ring |
| **swim** | swam | **swum** | a→u | 同 ring |
| **write** | wrote | **written** | 双t+en | wri-tten |

### 🟡 第二层：两形相同（过去式=过去分词）
> 这 15 个的过去式和过去分词一样，不会记混三形，但形式本身要记。

| 原形 | 过去式=过去分词 | 易错点 |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **bring** | brought | brought≠brang |
| **build** | built | built≠builded |
| **buy** | bought | bought≠buyed |
| **catch** | caught | caught≠catched |
| **feel** | felt | - |
| **fight** | fought | - |
| **find** | found | found≠finded |
| **have** | had | - |
| **keep** | kept | - |
| **lay** | laid | laid≠layed |
| **leave** | left | left≠leaved |
| **lend** | lent | - |
| **lose** | lost | lost≠losed |
| **make** | made | made≠maked |
| **teach** | taught | taught≠teached |

### 🟢 第三层：三形相同（最简单，快速过）
> 原形=过去式=过去分词，不用额外记。

| 原形 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **bet · broadcast · burst · cost · cut · fit · hit · hurt · let · put · quit · read** *(read/read/read 但发音不同)* · **set · shut · spread** |

### ⚠ 特殊注意
| 动词 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **come** | come → came → **come**（过去分词回到原形！≠came） |
| **run** | run → ran → **run**（同上） |
| **become** | become → became → **become** |
| **lie**(躺) | lie → lay → **lain**（与 lay 放 混淆，PET 高频陷阱） |
| **lay**(放) | lay → laid → **laid** |
| **hang** | hung/hanged 两形两义（hung=挂, hanged=绞死） |
| **learn** | learnt/learned 均可（英/美式） |
| **burn** | burnt/burned 均可 |

## 二、PET 高频固定搭配（按核心动词分类）

> Part 5 完形填空最爱考的就是搭配：**make/do/take/get/give/go/have + 名词**。
> 记住这些搭配，Part 5 正确率大幅提升。

### 🟠 make（制造/产生）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| make a decision | 做决定 | We need to **make a decision** today. |
| make a mistake | 犯错 | I **made a mistake** on the test. |
| make money | 赚钱 | She **makes money** selling cakes. |
| make a noise | 发出噪音 | Don't **make a noise** in the library. |
| make progress | 取得进步 | You've **made great progress** this term. |
| make an effort | 努力 | You need to **make more effort**. |
| make friends (with) | 交朋友 | He **made friends** quickly. |
| make sure | 确保 | **Make sure** you lock the door. |
| make up one's mind | 下定决心 | She **made up her mind** to study harder. |
| make a difference | 产生影响 | Recycling can **make a difference**. |

### 🔵 do（做/执行）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| do homework | 做作业 | I **do my homework** after dinner. |
| do business (with) | 做生意 | We **do business** with Japan. |
| do exercise | 锻炼 | You should **do more exercise**. |
| do your best | 尽力 | Just **do your best** in the exam. |
| do the dishes | 洗碗 | It's your turn to **do the dishes**. |
| do harm | 造成伤害 | Smoking **does harm** to your health. |
| do someone a favour | 帮忙 | Can you **do me a favour**? |

### 🟢 take（拿/花费/乘坐）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| take a photo | 拍照 | Let's **take a photo** here. |
| take medicine | 吃药 | **Take this medicine** twice a day. |
| take a bus/taxi/train | 乘公交/出租/火车 | I **take the bus** to school. |
| take care of | 照顾 | She **takes care of** her grandmother. |
| take part (in) | 参加 | Everyone **took part in** the game. |
| take place | 发生/举行 | The meeting **will take place** on Friday. |
| take time | 花时间 | Learning English **takes time**. |
| take advantage of | 利用 | **Take advantage of** this opportunity. |
| take it easy | 别紧张 | Hey, **take it easy**! |

### 🟡 get（得到/变得）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| get up | 起床 | I **get up** at 7 a.m. |
| get dressed | 穿衣服 | He **got dressed** quickly. |
| get lost | 迷路 | We **got lost** in the city. |
| get better | 好转/康复 | She's **getting better**. |
| get angry | 生气 | Don't **get angry** with me. |
| get in touch (with) | 联系 | I'll **get in touch** with you. |
| get rid of | 摆脱/除掉 | We need to **get rid of** old books. |
| get on/off | 上/下车 | **Get on** the bus at the next stop. |
| get together | 聚会 | Let's **get together** this weekend. |

### 🟣 give（给/产生）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| give up | 放弃 | Never **give up** on your dreams. |
| give advice | 给建议 | The teacher **gave us advice**. |
| give back | 归还 | Please **give back** my book. |
| give a hand | 帮忙 | Can you **give me a hand**? |
| give birth (to) | 生孩子 | She **gave birth to** twins. |
| give way | 让路/屈服 | **Give way** to oncoming traffic. |

### 🔴 go（去/变得）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| go shopping | 购物 | Let's **go shopping** this afternoon. |
| go ahead | 开始吧/去吧 | "**Go ahead**, I'll wait here." |
| go on | 继续 | Please **go on** with your story. |
| go out | 外出/熄灭 | The lights **went out**. |

### ⚪ have（有/做）
| 搭配 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|

## 三、动词+介词固定搭配（Part 6 开放填空高频）

| 动词/形容词 | + 介词 | 例 |
|------------|--------|-----|
| depend | **on** | It depends **on** the weather. |
| listen | **to** | I **listen to** music. |
| look **for** | 寻找 | I'm **looking for** my keys. |
| look **after** | 照顾 | She **looks after** children. |
| look **forward to** | 期待 | I **look forward to** seeing you. |
| be interested **in** | 对…感兴趣 | He is **interested in** science. |
| be good **at** | 擅长 | She is **good at** swimming. |
| be afraid **of** | 害怕 | I'm **afraid of** spiders. |
| be famous **for** | 因…出名 | Paris is **famous for** the Eiffel Tower. |
| belong **to** | 属于 | This book **belongs to** me. |
| concentrate **on** | 专注 | Please **concentrate on** your work. |
| suffer **from** | 患…病 | He **suffers from** headaches. |
| agree **with** | 同意(人) | I **agree with** you. |
| apologize **for** | 为…道歉 | I **apologize for** being late. |
| remind **of** | 使想起 | You **remind me of** your father. |

## 四、平台落地建议

### 方案：在语法工作台新增 2 个专项考点
1. **「不规则动词变形」专项**：三形全异 Top 20 + 两形相同 Top 15 = 35 个核心动词，每个出 3 题填空题（给原形→选过去式/过去分词），共 ~100 题。
2. **「固定搭配」专项**：make/do/take/get/give/go/have 七大动词 + 30 个其他高频搭配 = ~100 个搭配，每个出 2 题选词填空，共 ~200 题。

### 知识卡呈现
- 不规则动词：表格三列（原形→过去式→过去分词），标红易错形，🔊 发音
- 固定搭配：按动词分组，搭配+中文+例句🔊
- 练习题：「make __ a decision」选 a/the/up → "make a decision" 等
